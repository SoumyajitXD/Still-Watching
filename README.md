# 🩸 Still Watching

**Still Watching** is a **Forge 1.20.1 horror survival modpack** built around fog, sound, stalking entities, cursed exploration, brutal nights, and constant paranoia.

The Anomaly is watching.  
The night is not safe.  
Silence does not mean peace.

**Will you survive?**

---

## 📌 Project Overview

**Still Watching** transforms Minecraft into a hostile horror survival experience where comfort dies quickly.

This is not a cozy survival pack with a few scary mobs thrown in like cheap plastic skeletons. This modpack is designed to make the world feel wrong. Fog hides movement. Sounds matter. Structures are dangerous. Darkness is not empty. Something is always watching from just outside the torchlight.

The pack focuses on:

- Cinematic horror atmosphere
- Stalking horror entities
- Sound-based fear and awareness
- Dangerous exploration
- Cursed structures and dungeons
- Multiplayer horror survival
- Fog, darkness, paranoia, and dread
- Survival over power fantasy

---

## 🧟 Main Hook

> Enter a Forge 1.20.1 horror survival world where **The Anomaly** stalks the fog, deadly nights punish every mistake, and silence never means safety.

## 🔥 Features

### 🕯️ Cinematic Horror Atmosphere

Still Watching uses horror-focused visuals, dynamic lighting, ambient sound, audio physics, screen effects, and dark environmental design to make Minecraft feel hostile.

Even when nothing is attacking you, the world should feel unsafe.

---

### 🌫️ Fog, Darkness & Paranoia

Fog is not decoration here.  
Darkness is not empty.  
The silence is not friendly.

Expect strange sounds, distant movement, fake safety, and moments where you are not sure if the danger is real or already behind you.

---

### 👹 Stalking Horror Entities

The pack includes terrifying creatures that hunt, observe, ambush, mimic, and punish careless players.

Some enemies charge at you.  
Some wait.  
Some watch first.

That is worse.

---

### 🔊 Sound-Based Fear

Audio awareness matters.

Footsteps, echoes, whispers, growls, knocks, and distant noises can all signal danger. Playing without sound is basically donating your organs to the cave.

**Headphones are strongly recommended.**

---

### 🌲 Cursed World Generation

The world is expanded with darker terrain, eerie biomes, abandoned locations, twisted structures, and brutal dungeons.

Exploration should feel like breaking into somewhere you were never supposed to find.

---

### 🏚️ Exploration With Consequences

Loot is not free.  
Structures are not safe.  
Dungeons fight back.

Curiosity can reward you, but it can also drag you directly into regret.

---

### 🔫 Survival, Not Power Fantasy

Weapons and guns exist, but they do not make you invincible.

Ammo can run out.  
Panic ruins aim.  
Gear does not save stupid decisions.

Survival depends on awareness, preparation, movement, and knowing when to run.

---

### 🎭 Unpredictable Nights

No routine is completely safe.

Some nights are quiet.  
Some are violent.  
Some are worse because nothing happens until it does.

The worst mistake is trusting the silence.

---

## 👁️ The Iconic Threat: The Anomaly

**The Anomaly** is the signature nightmare of Still Watching.

It is something that should not exist, but does anyway. Reality feels unstable around it. Safety becomes temporary. The rules stop feeling reliable.

You do not simply fight The Anomaly.

You endure it.

---

## ☠️ Featured Threats

Still Watching includes a roster of hostile and terrifying entities designed to make the world feel hunted, haunted, and deeply unsafe.

### The Anomaly

The signature creature of the pack. A reality-breaking presence that makes the world feel unstable and wrong.

### The Man From The Fog

A relentless stalker in the mist. If the fog feels too quiet, it probably is not.

### Apollyon

A destructive nightmare that turns survival into panic. Running may be smarter than fighting.

### The Knocker

A sound-based horror that turns distant knocks into pure dread.

### Herobrine

The classic Minecraft myth returns with strange sightings, eerie structures, and glowing eyes in the dark.

### The Mimicer

A creature built around betrayal and uncertainty. If something familiar feels wrong, trust that feeling.

### The Goatman

A forest nightmare that waits where the woods become too dark.

### The Midnight Lurker

A creature that thrives in darkness and punishes players who stop paying attention.

### Siren Head

A towering horror that weaponizes sound, distance, and panic.

### Cave Dweller Reimagined

Mining is no longer peaceful. The underground is alive, and it hates visitors.
