STILL WATCHING

Forge 1.20.1 Horror Survival built around fog, sound, stalking entities, cursed exploration, and constant paranoia.

The Anomaly is watching. The night is not safe. Will you survive?

Still Watching is a cinematic Minecraft horror modpack designed to destroy comfort. This is not cozy survival with a few monsters sprinkled on top. This is a hostile world of fog-covered forests, abandoned structures, distorted sounds, brutal nights, and creatures that stalk, hunt, mimic, and wait. Build carefully. Explore slowly. Listen closely. Something is always wrong.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT?

🌑 CINEMATIC HORROR ATMOSPHERE
Ambient sounds, realistic audio physics, dynamic lighting, screen distortion, blood effects, and horror-focused visuals turn Minecraft into a world that feels unsafe even when nothing is attacking you.

🌫️ FOG, DARKNESS & PARANOIA
The fog hides movement. The dark hides mistakes. Distant sounds, strange events, and fake safety keep you guessing whether you are alone — or being watched.

👹 STALKING HORROR ENTITIES
Face terrifying creatures that hunt, observe, ambush, mimic, and punish careless players. Some threats do not simply attack. They wait for the perfect moment.

🔊 SOUND MATTERS
Every footstep, echo, growl, whisper, and distant noise can mean danger. Headphones are highly recommended — unless you enjoy being jumpscared like an idiot in a cave.

🌲 CURSED WORLD GENERATION
Explore overhauled terrain, darker biomes, abandoned towns, eerie structures, and unforgiving dungeons. Every location feels like somewhere you were never supposed to enter.

🏚️ EXPLORATION WITH CONSEQUENCES
Loot is never free. Structures can become traps. Dungeons fight back. Curiosity can reward you — or drag you straight into regret.

🔫 SURVIVAL, NOT POWER FANTASY
Weapons and guns exist, but they do not make you invincible. Ammo is limited, panic ruins aim, and the strongest gear still means nothing if fear makes you stupid.

🎭 UNPREDICTABLE NIGHTS
No routine is completely safe. Some nights are quiet. Some are not. The worst mistake you can make is assuming the silence means peace.

THE THREATS

These are not decorations. These are the things that turn your world into a mistake.

THE ANOMALY

The Anomaly is the signature nightmare of Still Watching — something that should not exist, but does anyway. Reality feels unstable around it. Safety becomes temporary. The rules stop feeling reliable. You do not simply fight The Anomaly. You endure it.

THE MAN FROM THE FOG

A relentless presence in the mist. The Man From The Fog stalks from the edge of vision, turns distance into panic, and makes every foggy biome feel like a hunting ground.

APOLLYON

Apollyon is destruction given form. When it appears, survival stops being a plan and becomes a desperate reaction. Face it only when running is no longer enough.

THE KNOCKER

The Knocker turns sound into fear. Its hollow knocks echo through the dark, warning you that something is close — or that it has already found you.

HEROBRINE

The classic Minecraft myth returns as a constant reminder that your world is not yours anymore. Strange structures, distant sightings, and glowing eyes turn nostalgia into dread.

THE MIMICER

The Mimicer turns trust into a weakness. When something familiar starts behaving wrong, you have already waited too long.

THE GOATMAN

The Goatman waits where the woods go black. Forests, bridges, and lonely paths become hunting grounds once it starts following your trail.

THE MIDNIGHT LURKER

The Midnight Lurker thrives in darkness. Ignore it and it closes the distance. Notice it too late and the night becomes a sprint for your life.

SIREN HEAD

Siren Head weaponizes sound, distance, and panic. If you hear something unnatural echoing through the trees, do not investigate. That is how idiots become warnings.

CAVE DWELLER REIMAGINED

Mining is no longer peaceful. The Cave Dweller Reimagined stalks underground darkness, turning every echo, tunnel, and shadow into a reason to leave immediately.

RECOMMENDED SETUP

Minecraft: 1.20.1
Mod Loader: Forge
Recommended RAM: 5GB or higher
Best Experience: Headphones recommended
Multiplayer: Supported — horror is worse when your friends start screaming too.

FINAL WARNING

Still Watching is made for players who want Minecraft to feel dangerous, unpredictable, and wrong. If you want comfort, build a cottage somewhere else. If you want dread, step into the fog.

Enter the world. Listen carefully. Do not trust the silence.
